Purpose Alignment

Aligning individual purpose with a company's mission is one of the most powerful drivers of organizational performance. Employees with a strong sense of purpose are 5.6 times more likely to be engaged, and a 10% improvement in mission connection correlates with a 4.4% increase in profitability and an 8.1% decrease in turnover. Yet only 18% of U.S. employees believe in the purpose of their work, making this gap not just a culture problem but a competitive one. Closing it is how organizations build cultures where employees feel valued for who they are, not just what they produce.
