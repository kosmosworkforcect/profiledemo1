Leveraging Strengths

Every employee brings a distinct set of innate strengths, and when those strengths are recognized and deliberately paired, teams become greater than the sum of their parts. Gallup research confirms this at scale: employees who learn to use their strengths are 7.8% more productive, and teams that actively focus on strengths daily see 12.5% greater productivity. The compounding effect of complementary strengths also drives retention, with strengths-aware teams showing 22 times less active disengagement than teams where strengths aren’t appreciated. Gallup's study of 11,441 teams found that a team's awareness of its own strengths had twice the influence on performance as the specific composition of those strengths, meaning visibility is the catalyst. Organizations that build cultures around strengths identification do not just unlock individual potential; they engineer the conditions for teams to thrive together.
