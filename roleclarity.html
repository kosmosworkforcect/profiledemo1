Role Clarity

For many employees, career growth stalls not from lack of ambition, but lack of clarity. Without a clear understanding of which skills to develop now, which to build toward their next role, and which paths are actually available to them, even high-potential employees disengage. Research bears this out: 54% of employees feel entirely on their own when it comes to advancing their careers, and one in four say they are likely to leave their organization within six months due to insufficient development support. Yet the cost of inaction is largely self-inflicted. Over 90% of employees say they would not quit if given meaningful development opportunities, and organizations that invest in clear, structured career pathways see measurable gains in retention, productivity, and performance. The missing ingredient is rarely motivation. It is visibility.
